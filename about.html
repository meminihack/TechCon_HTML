History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the vision to bring together the brightest minds in technology to discuss the future of the industry. Over the past decade, TechCon has grown into one of the leading technology conferences in the world, known for its cutting-edge sessions and influential speakers.

Our Mission

At TechCon 2024, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the technology sector. We aim to provide a platform for professionals, enthusiasts, and leaders to exchange ideas, explore new technologies, and drive the future of tech.
